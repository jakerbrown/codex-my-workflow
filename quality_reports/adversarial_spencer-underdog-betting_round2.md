# Adversarial Review Round 2: Spencer underdog betting

## Recheck after revisions

The revised package now handles the main attack lines better:

- the season-proxy caveat is visible
- the mechanism language is softer and more defensible
- the refined variant is framed as exploratory
- the simulation is not presented as calibrated proof

## Residual objections

1. The archive still stops in 2021.
2. The project still relies on a public archival source rather than an official
   multi-league betting feed.
3. Some readers may still treat "better than all underdogs" as stronger than it
   really is.

## Verdict

The residual objections are limitations, not fatal flaws. The final conclusion
remains appropriately skeptical:

- no robust cross-league money-making edge
- weak hint of mild early mispricing in a subset of leagues
