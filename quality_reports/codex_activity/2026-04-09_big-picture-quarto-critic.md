# Big Picture Quarto critic review

I reviewed the Big Picture podcast blog draft, its rendered HTML, and the
supporting output files without editing the project files.

The main issues were that the lead sentiment claim was stronger than the linked
model supported, the published sample sizes were stale relative to newer output
artifacts, the movie commonality table did not match the current CSV, and the
movie-title extraction still treated people and text fragments as movies.

This mattered because the current draft reads more confident and more current
than the shipped evidence justifies.

I verified the QMD, HTML, figures, and key CSV outputs and wrote a critic
report plus final QA report. The draft remains not approved pending revision.
