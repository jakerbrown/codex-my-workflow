# Pedagogy Review: Spencer underdog betting

## Narrative assessment

The brief is understandable to an informed non-specialist audience. The order
is sensible and the bottom-line message is easy to retain.

## Strengths

- Opens with the question before methods.
- Introduces the data and design before results.
- Ends with the conclusion rather than with technical caveats.

## Weaknesses

- The jump from headline ROI to league heterogeneity is abrupt.
- A one-line bridge such as "the pooled result hides meaningful league
  differences" would improve narrative continuity.

## Verdict

- No pedagogical blocker found.
- Quality from a pedagogy perspective: `89-90`.
